About Us
Mind Setter is an online learning platform developed by Team Crusaders to provide a better learning experience for Sri Lankan students in grades 10 to 13. Our platform offers a personalized study plan, knowledge testing, and access to video tutorials by various teachers. With the use of AI algorithms, we ensure that each student receives a tailored learning experience that caters to their specific needs. At Mind Setter, we are committed to improving the education system in Sri Lanka and providing quality education to all students, regardless of their socio-economic background or location.
